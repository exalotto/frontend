## Privacy Policy

Welcome to ExaLotto! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle
information on our website.

### Key Highlights

- **No data collection**: we do not collect, process, or store any personal data.
- **No Cookies**: our website does not use cookies.
- **No Third-Party Trackers**: our website contains no ads, analytics, or third-party tracking
  tools.
- **Decentralized Interactions**: all interactions are handled directly via Web3 technology and the
  [Polygon PoS blockchain][polygon-pos] using your wallet.

### Information We Do Not Collect

- Personal information (e.g., name, email, address).
- Device or browsing data (e.g., IP address, location, or user behavior).

### No Use of Cookies

This website does not set or use cookies of any kind.

### Blockchain Interactions

This website interacts directly with the Ethereum blockchain via [Web3.js][web3js]:

- Your wallet address is used solely for blockchain transactions.
- We do not store or process any wallet information on our servers (there are no servers).

Please note: blockchain transactions are public, immutable, and outside the scope of this Privacy
Policy.

### Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, as we do not collect any data.
