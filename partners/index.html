ExaLotto Partnership Program

Overview

Anyone can make money with ExaLotto, and not just by winning!

ExaLotto is managed as a DAO ("Decentralized Autonomous Organization") and anyone can join. Voting power is measured in EXL, an ERC20⤴ token that you can trade on Uniswap⤴. Proposals and votes are managed on our Tally page⤴.

Referral Program

Want to make even more money with ExaLotto? We run a referral program allowing anyone to sell ExaLotto tickets and take a generous 10% cut on their sales!

Joining the program is easy as 1-2-3.
